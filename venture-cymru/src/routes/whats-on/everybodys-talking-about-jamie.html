Summary

In "Everybody's Talking About Jamie," sixteen-year-old Jamie New—a resident of a Sheffield council estate—tells the tale of his struggles with fearing the future and not fitting in. With the help of his devoted mother and friends, Jamie overcomes discrimination and bullies to take center stage and become a celebrity. Rated 'funny, provocative, touching' by the Daily Telegraph, this musical is appropriate for all ages. Ivano Turco plays Jamie, Shobna Gulati plays Ray, Rebecca McKinnis plays Margaret, and Talia Palamathanan plays Pritti. Other prominent casting is still pending.
