About Venue Cymru

Welcome to Venue Cymru, a Theatre in Llandudno, Wales. We host a wide variety of live performances (such as musicals and theatrical productions), films, and more! We aim to provide an easily available, fun day out for the family.

Over the many years we've been in service, we have made a great cultural impact in the UK, even dating back as far as the original Llandudno Promenade Theatre in 1894 (that's 0 years)!

History

Venue Cymru is has a very rich history over several decades, and we've managed to become a hub or sorts for entertainment in Wales.

Origin

Our roots can be traced all the way back to the Llandudno Promenade Theatre, originally built in 1894. The theatre underwent several transformations over many years and hosted many performances for people to enjoy.

Modernisation

The 20th century is when the theatre was greatly modernised. With growing demands of a diverse range of events, we had to revolutionise the theatre. After the revamp, we re-opened under the name Venue Cymru in 2007, as we had far expanded beyond our original goal of just traditional theatre performances.
